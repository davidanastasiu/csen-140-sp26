In this lab, you will get familiar with running AI/ML workloads on the Santa Clara University HPC.

First, go to the AI/ML on the HPC course on Canvas (https://camino.instructure.com/courses/116808). Once you visit the course page, you first have to click the "+ Join this Course" button on the right to join the course.

Next, go through all the videos and quizzes in the course, one module at a time. The next module will only be available once you pass the quiz from the current module. At the end of the course, you will receive a certificate. Download or print to PDF the certificate and upload it to the lab assignment to complete the lab.

Unlike all other labs, which are due at the end of the lab class time, this lab is due Sunday night at 11:59 pm.
